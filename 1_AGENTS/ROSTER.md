## Collaboration Patterns

### For Landing Page Builds
`ux-researcher` (brief analysis) → `ui-ux-designer` (design + build) → `frontend-motion-designer` (animations) → `accessibility-auditor` (final check)

### For UX Audits
`ux-researcher` (heuristic evaluation) + `accessibility-auditor` (a11y check) → combined report

### For Brand Identity
`ux-researcher` (market research) → `brand-identity-designer` (palette + identity) → `ui-ux-designer` (apply to UI)
